# Food Guidelines

### Nutrition Levels

|Nutrition Level|Energy Amount (cal/day)|
|:---:|:---:|
|1|1800-2399|
|2|2400-2999|
|3|3000+|

Choose nutrition level:
* Body weight x 10 = resting metabolic rate (RMR)
* RMR x 0.2 = daily activity burn (DAB)
* 600 = P90X workouts
* Energy level = RMR + DAB + P90X
